About Remine India

Remine India is a startup that specializes in the responsible recycling of electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries. Our mission is to promote sustainable development and reduce environmental pollution by transforming waste into valuable resources. With our state-of-the-art technology and skilled team, we offer comprehensive e-waste management solutions that are safe, secure, and compliant with regulatory standards.
